Target Audience

The target audience for this website are members of all ages from the local community who want to discover a good web service that provides weather information for their area as well as information about local events.

Most users will usually access the web site through their mobile device, but there will also be users accessing it from a computer. Statistically, most users will be economically active and with at least some college education. There will also be users that require the weather information to complete their every-day activities.

Scenarios

#1: Sandra is a young girls who studies in the university. Her car broke down and she needs to take the bus. She needs the weather information urgently to be prepared for the trip to the university.

#2: Monica likes to run during the afternoon but the day is cloudy. She does not want to go out without being prepared, so, she took a look at the weather info.

#3: Eduardo is an English teacher who was recently teaching from home. The school decided to go back to the building and he want to be cautious.

#4: Kelly works as a remote tech support agent and her colleagues invited her to a meeting. He checked the weather before going out.

#5: Luis has to wash his car, but he wants to know if the weather will help before applying the car shining polish.
